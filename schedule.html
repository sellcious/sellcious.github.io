When We Practice

All classes are held at World Class Martial Arts in Boothwyn, PA and are open to all levels. New students are welcome to observe any class. Contact us to arrange your first visit!

Friday

8:00 PM – 9:30 PM | Ki-Aikido (All Levels)

Our Friday evening class covers the full range of Ki-Aikido practice including warm-ups, Ki development exercises, individual forms (hitori waza), paired techniques, and weapons work. All levels are welcome from beginners to advanced students.

All ages, all levels. A great starting point for beginners and a well-rounded practice for experienced aikidoka.

Sunday

9:30 AM – 12:00 PM | Ki-Aikido (All Levels)

Our extended Sunday morning session offers a deep dive into Ki-Aikido training. With extra time on the mat, we explore Ki principles, technique refinement, and advanced applications at a relaxed but focused pace. A wonderful way to center your weekend.

All ages, all levels. The longer format allows for more detailed instruction and exploration.
