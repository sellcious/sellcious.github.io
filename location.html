Visiting Us

Observing a Class

Visitors are always welcome to observe any of our regularly scheduled classes. This is a great way to get a feel for our practice and community before joining. No appointment needed; just come by a few minutes before class begins.

Parking Information

Free parking is available at the facility. The parking lot is located directly in front of World Class Martial Arts.

Please arrive 10–15 minutes before class to allow time for parking and preparation.

Your First Visit

New students are welcome! Contact us before your first visit so we can answer any questions and ensure you have everything you need. Wear comfortable clothing you can move freely in, and bring an open mind.

For more details about what to expect, visit our Ki-Aikido page.
